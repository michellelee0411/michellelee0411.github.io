My Favorite Car, the Porsche

This website is a brief overview of my favorite car, the Porsche. When I was a young boy, around 9 or 10, I learned to fix cars. My neighbor had a 1963 Porsche 356 1600. It was a thing of beauty. He was only 17 and quite knowledgeable about working on cars. He taught me. Obviously, I did not have my own car to work on but it was a love and a skill that I still have today.
